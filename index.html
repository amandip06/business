🍰 Our Story

Made with Love, Served with Joy

Welcome to Prince Home Made Cakes and Chocolates — a passionate home bakery founded by Arpita Pradip Singh. What started as a heartfelt desire to spread happiness through homemade sweets has blossomed into a beloved brand trusted by hundreds of families.

Every single item is crafted by hand using the freshest ingredients — no preservatives, no shortcuts. We believe that when food is made with care, it carries a special energy that makes every bite memorable. From elaborate custom cakes to delicate handmade chocolates, we pour our heart into every creation.
